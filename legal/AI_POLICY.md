# AI Policy

**Effective Date:** 2026-02-23

**Helmetsan**, operated by **Ash Digital Services**, uses artificial intelligence (AI) in limited ways to improve our content and services. This policy explains where and how we use AI, what we do not do with it, and how you can reach us with questions or corrections.

## Our Use of AI

We use AI to:

- **Improve content and usability** — For example, to help generate or refine meta descriptions and other SEO-related text for product and brand pages, and to fill in missing product data (e.g. shell material, category) where we have structured context. We do **not** use AI to replace human judgment on safety, legal compliance, or certification accuracy.

## Where AI Is Used

### SEO (Meta Descriptions and Titles)

We may use AI to generate or assist in generating meta titles and meta descriptions for helmets, brands, and accessories. This helps our pages be more discoverable in search engines. These descriptions are based on existing product data and are reviewed for appropriateness. They are not presented as human-authored in a way that would mislead you; they are standard SEO metadata.

### Data Enrichment

We may use AI to fill in empty fields in our product and brand data (e.g. shell material, head shape options, category, or brand story) when we have sufficient structured context. Where possible, we apply validation rules and human review. This helps keep our catalog complete and consistent.

### Future Uses

We may introduce additional AI-powered tools (e.g. data quality checks, integrity checks, or other internal or user-facing features). When we add **material** new uses of AI that affect how we present or use information, we will update this policy and the effective date.

## What We Do Not Do

- We do **not** present AI-generated content as if it were written by a named human author without disclosure where that would be misleading.
- We do **not** use AI to provide legal, medical, or safety advice. Safety and legality information (e.g. certifications, road legality) are based on manufacturer and certification data and our research, not on AI-generated advice.
- We do **not** use AI for automated decision-making about individuals in a way that would significantly affect their rights (e.g. we do not use AI to profile users for credit or employment decisions).

## Providers and Data

Our AI features may use third-party AI providers (e.g. Groq, Google Gemini, or other providers we configure). We send only the data necessary for the task (e.g. product context, field names, existing metadata). We do **not** send your personal information to AI providers for these content/data tasks unless we have a separate, disclosed use. Each provider's handling of data is subject to their privacy policy and terms; we choose providers with appropriate data practices.

## Quality and Accuracy

We aim for accuracy in all content, including where AI has assisted. However, we do **not** guarantee that AI-generated or AI-assisted output is error-free. You should verify important information (e.g. specifications, certifications, pricing) with manufacturers or retailers when it matters for your purchase or safety decisions. Our [Disclaimer](disclaimer) applies to all content on our site.

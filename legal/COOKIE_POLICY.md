# Cookie Policy

**Effective Date:** 2026-02-23

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that a website or its service provider places on your computer or device through your web browser. They allow the site's or service provider's systems to recognize your browser and capture and remember certain information (for example, your preferences or what pages you have visited). Cookies can be "first-party" (set by us) or "third-party" (set by a service we use, such as an analytics provider).

## Who We Are and Scope

This Cookie Policy applies to the websites and mobile applications operated by Helmetsan (including helmetsan.com and related domains). It describes the types of cookies and similar technologies we use, why we use them, and how you can control them.

## Types of Cookies We Use

### Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, load balancing, and remembering your preferences (e.g. consent choices). You cannot opt out of these cookies if you wish to use the site.

### Functional Cookies

These cookies allow the website to remember choices you make (such as your preferred language or region) and to provide enhanced, more personal features. They may be set by us or by third-party providers whose services we have added to our pages.

### Analytics and Performance Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. We use this information to improve how the site works and to improve the user experience. For example, we may use **Google Analytics** to analyze traffic, measure effectiveness of content, and understand user behavior. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect information such as your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on the site. You can opt out of Google Analytics via the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

### Marketing / Advertising Cookies (if applicable)

If we use advertising or remarketing services, cookies may be placed by us or our advertising partners to build a profile of your interests and show you relevant advertisements on other sites. You can often control these via your browser or via the third party's preference tools (e.g. [Google Ads Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads)).

## How We Obtain Consent

Where required by law, we obtain your consent before placing non-essential cookies on your device. This may be through a cookie banner or preference center when you first visit our site. You can change your preferences at any time (e.g. via the same banner or your browser settings). Continuing to use our website after we have shown the banner may be treated as consent where applicable under local law.

## Managing Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your device and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed. For more information, see [aboutcookies.org](https://www.aboutcookies.org) or [allaboutcookies.org](https://www.allaboutcookies.org). These sites explain how to manage cookies in common browsers (e.g. Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge). If you block or delete cookies, some features of our website may not function properly (for example, saved preferences or parts of the site that rely on analytics).

## Do Not Track and Global Opt-Out

We do not currently respond to browser "Do Not Track" (DNT) signals in a specific way. You can limit tracking by disabling or opting out of cookies as described above. For Google Analytics, you can use the [Google Analytics Opt-out add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout). For more information on Do Not Track, visit [allaboutdnt.com](https://allaboutdnt.com/).

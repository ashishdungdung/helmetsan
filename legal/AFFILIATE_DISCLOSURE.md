**Effective Date:** 2026-02-23

**Helmetsan**, operated by **Ash Digital Services**, is committed to transparency. This page explains how we may earn money when you use our site and how that affects the content you see.

## What Is Affiliate Marketing?

Affiliate marketing is a way for websites to earn a commission when visitors click on links to products or retailers and make a purchase (or sometimes take another qualifying action). The price you pay the retailer is **the same** whether you use our link or go directly to the store. We do not charge you extra for using our links.

## Our Participation in Affiliate Programs

We participate in affiliate programs offered by retailers and marketplaces. When you click on a "Where to Buy," "Buy Now," or similar link on our site and then make a qualifying purchase on the retailer's site, we may receive a commission or other compensation. This helps us run Helmetsan and keep providing comparisons, reviews, and product information.

Programs we may participate in include (but are not limited to):

- **Amazon Associates** — When you shop on Amazon (e.g. Amazon.com, Amazon.in, Amazon.co.uk) via our links, we may earn from qualifying purchases. Our site may use an affiliate tag (e.g. helmetsan-20) in links to Amazon.
- **Flipkart Affiliate Programme** — For visitors in India, we may use Flipkart affiliate links so we can earn when you make qualifying purchases on Flipkart.
- **Other retailer and marketplace programmes** — We may join or use links from other affiliate or referral programmes (e.g. other regional marketplaces or partner programmes). Where those links appear, the same principle applies: we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you.

The list of programmes and the presence of affiliate links may change over time. We will update this disclosure when we add or remove material programmes.

- On helmet and accessory pages, we may show **"Where to Buy"** or **"Buy Now"** buttons and links that take you to retailers (e.g. Amazon, Flipkart, or other partners). Those links may be affiliate links.

Our decision to include or recommend a product is not based on whether we have an affiliate relationship with a retailer that sells it.
